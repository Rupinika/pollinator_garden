Featured Pollinator Plants

Roses

Classic garden favorites that attract bees and butterflies. Choose single-petal varieties for better pollinator access.

Growing Tips: Plant in well-drained soil, water deeply but infrequently, and prune in early spring.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 2-6 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Dandelions

Early spring bloomers that provide crucial food for bees. Their bright yellow flowers are a favorite of many pollinators.

Growing Tips: No special care needed - they grow naturally! Let them flower before mowing.

Bloom Time: Spring to Fall

Height: 6-12 inches

Sun Requirements: Full Sun to Partial Shade

Sunflowers

Large, bright flowers that attract bees, butterflies, and birds. Great for adding height to your garden.

Growing Tips: Plant in full sun, water regularly, and provide support for tall varieties.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 3-12 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Marigolds

Easy-to-grow annuals that attract bees and butterflies. Their bright colors make them garden favorites.

Growing Tips: Start from seed or transplants, deadhead regularly, and water when soil is dry.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 1-3 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Zinnias

Colorful annuals that bloom all summer long. A favorite of butterflies and hummingbirds.

Growing Tips: Direct sow after last frost, deadhead regularly, and water at the base.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 1-3 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Cosmos

Delicate flowers that attract bees and butterflies. Easy to grow from seed and self-sows readily.

Growing Tips: Direct sow in spring, thin seedlings, and water during dry spells.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 2-4 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Lavender

A fragrant perennial that attracts bees and butterflies with its purple blooms. Drought-resistant and perfect for sunny spots.

Growing Tips: Plant in well-drained soil, prune in spring, and avoid overwatering.

Bloom Time: Summer

Height: 1-3 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Milkweed

Essential for monarch butterflies and supports various pollinators. Host plant for monarch caterpillars.

Growing Tips: Plant in full sun, water regularly until established, and leave seed pods for winter interest.

Bloom Time: Summer

Height: 2-4 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun

Echinacea

Beautiful coneflowers that attract butterflies and support native bees. Long blooming season.

Growing Tips: Plant in well-drained soil, deadhead for continuous bloom, and divide every few years.

Bloom Time: Summer to Fall

Height: 2-4 feet

Sun Requirements: Full Sun
